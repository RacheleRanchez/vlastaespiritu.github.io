ABOUT US

In 2003, what started out a hobby-included business in San Fernando, La Union, expandedinto a proudly filipino-owned and professionally run business. Coffee House has grown to over 250 outlets all over the Philippines and over 30 stores in US with locations in California, Washington, Hawaii, New York, and Virginia. With its acquistion in October 2017 by Starbucks Food Corporation, Coffee House entered a new phase in its company history. The company soon became one of the biggest and fastest growing cafes in the Philippines and taken part in every Filipino's special moments.
